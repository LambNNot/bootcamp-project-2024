The Natural Deities

Hello World! This is my website.

The Natural Deities of Magnolia are manifests of the people’s creative lifeforce, their Viessence. Little does the common-man know that although the Gods establish rule of their own will, they are granted their strengths by those of their worshippers. It was once believed that the death of a natural deity was no more than the cessation of their worship. What Magnolian residents aptly call Will, the elemental magic of their day-to-day lives, is not solely bestowed upon them by some higher power but rather a realization of their own attunement with the different elements of nature.

However, the folk tales of the Magnolian world will teach that these powers are gifts of the Gods of Nature and that one’s mastery over Will is tied to their faith. It is for this reason that those who have yet exercised or are ungifted with Will are called Acredo, meaning "without faith".
